Place the artwork on your wall

This custom version opens the camera first and waits for you to choose the wall. It avoids creating the artwork automatically on the first detected surface.

Use Android Chrome on HTTPS. For blank white walls, use Manual Place after pointing the camera at the desired spot.
Auto mode: point at a textured wall until the outline appears, then tap Place on Wall.
Blank wall mode: if the wall has no visual reference, tap Manual Place. This places the artwork in front of the camera at a fixed distance and keeps it in world space.
